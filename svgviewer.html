✦ SVG STUDIO GUIDE

Mastering SVG Studio V3.2

Welcome to the ultimate vector environment. SVG Studio is designed for high-performance editing, AI-assisted creation, and professional motion design. Here’s your complete operational manual:

✦ AI Assistance & Generation

Use the ✦ AI ASSIST panel to generate SVGs from natural language. Our Gemini 2.5 Flash engine is optimized for raw XML output.

  • Explain Code: Highlight code or click 'Explain' to get a structural breakdown.
  • Custom Key: Add your Gemini API key in Settings for high-limit usage.

⚡ Motion & FX

Bring your vectors to life without writing JavaScript. Use the Animate dropdown for instant GSAP/SMIL presets.

  • Motion FX: Glitch, Neon, Pulse, and Liquid morphing effects.
  • Z-Index Control: Use the Layers panel to manage depth and overlap.

📦 Assets & Smart Tools

Accelerate your design with modular assets and utility functions.

  • Asset Library: High-end, curated Y2K and Cyber shapes injected directly into code.
  • Remove BG: Strip background elements instantly via the context menu or toolbar.
  • Color Extractor: Automatically extract a palette from your current SVG.

📤 High-Fidelity Export

Export your work in any format. Perfect for web, social, or professional production.

  • PNG & SVG: Standard high-res pixel or vector exports.
  • Looping GIF: Optimized for sharing and web loops.
  • WebM Recording: Record your animations in high-def video.
