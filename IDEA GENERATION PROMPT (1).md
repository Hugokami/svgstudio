\- Output ONLY the niches and their titles.  
\- No explanations, no commentary, no references.  
\- Clean formatting.  
Generate unique ideas only.  
**SCRIPT GENERATION PROMPT**  
Act as a professional explainer video script writer.  
I will provide a VIDEO TITLE or VIDEO IDEA.  
INPUT:  
Video Title / Idea: **\[PASTE TITLE OR IDEA HERE\]**  
YOUR TASK:  
Generate a full explainer-style script that breaks the topic into multiple TYPES / ITEMS / CASES and explains them one by one.  
ABSOLUTE STRUCTURE RULES:  
\- Start the script IMMEDIATELY with the first item.  
\- NO introduction, NO hook, NO greetings, NO channel mentions.  
\- EVERY new item MUST begin a new paragraph.  
\- The FIRST sentence of EVERY paragraph MUST be ONLY the NAME of the item being explained, followed by a period.  
\- Do NOT describe, hint at, or imply an item before naming it.  
\- Explanation MUST come only AFTER the item name is written.  
NARRATION STYLE (VERY IMPORTANT):  
\- Write like a calm human narrator, not an AI.  
\- Use natural sentence flow and varied sentence length.  
\- Avoid repetitive sentence patterns.  
\- Avoid robotic phrasing such as repeatedly using “This is because…”.  
\- Use occasional soft transitions naturally within explanations.  
LANGUAGE RULES:  
\- Simple, clear, easy-to-narrate English.  
\- No academic tone.  
\- No slang, jokes, hype, or clickbait phrases.  
\- No bullet points, no lists, no headings.  
CONTENT GUIDELINES:  
\- Explain what the item is, where it comes from, what makes it different, and why it matters.  
\- Include light historical or real-world context when relevant.  
\- Keep each item similar in length unless naturally necessary.  
\- Maintain steady pacing suitable for narration.  
ENDING RULE:  
\- End naturally after the final item.  
\- NO outro, NO call to action.  
OUTPUT RULES:  
\- Output ONLY the script text.  
\- No formatting, no explanations, no meta commentary.
